Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's what you need to know.

⚠️
This action is permanent

Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to proceed before submitting a request.

What Will Be Deleted

When you delete your OneShake account, the following data is permanently removed:

  • 👤 Your profile, username, display name, and avatar
  • 🥃 All drink reviews, ratings, and tasting notes
  • 📸 All uploaded photos (review images and profile picture)
  • 💬 All comments you've made on other reviews
  • 👥 Your friends list and all friend connections
  • 🔔 All notification history and preferences
  • 🏆 Your leaderboard rank and cheers given/received

How to Delete Your Account

Send an email to our support team with your account details:

  1. Send an email to [email protected]
  2. Use the subject line: "Delete OneShake Account"
  3. Include the email address associated with your OneShake account
  4. Optionally, include your username so we can locate your account faster
✉️ Send Deletion Request
🕐

Account deletion requests are processed within 48 hours. You'll receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed. During this period, your account remains active.

Questions?

If you're having issues with the app or your account, we'd love to help before you delete. Reach out to us at [email protected] and we'll do our best to resolve any concerns.
